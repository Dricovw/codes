How I made my studying choice:

When I finished my high school with my specialisation in economics,

Yes, a specialisation in economics. The year I chose the profile, I got an Ipad to help my terrible handwriting.

Let's just say the writing improved, but the motivation went a different way.

So I setteld for a non-grade required specialisation: economics

And after graduating with the profile, I didn't want to spend my life looking at graphs and inflation rates.

And because of the reasons on my homepage, I decided a technical study was a better option for me.

Then I sorted by the best option the study-choice-site could give me for the study I wanted to do.

That was a certain University called 'HZ University of Applied sciences'.

I went to the open day and the tryout programs and liked the style and feeling of HZ.

After visiting a few other uni's I decided that I should try the ICT study at HZ.

When I graduate and start working for a company, I have no clue what sector of IT I shall join, so I just have to figure it out in a couple years.

During the study I expect to learn how to code with different and learn how to make websites, applications and programs.

Let's see how that is going to go.
